About
Galbi Dak

Galbidak is a halal Korean restaurant and barbecue in Tangier, offering authentic dishes like dak-galbi (spicy stir-fried chicken) in a setting that reflects South Korean culture and music. Founded by a South Korean who settled in Morocco, it has become popular among both tourists and locals.
